Depression

Major depression is a common illnes that severely limits psychosocial functioning and diminishes quality of life. In 2008, WHO ranked major depression as the third cause of burden of disease worldwide and projected that the disease will rank first by 2030. In practice, its detection, diagnosis and management often pose challenges for clinicians because of its various presentations, unpredictable course and prognosis, and variable response to treatment.
